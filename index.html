About me

Hi! I'm Paulina, and I've been polyamorous since I was 15. This page covers the basics of polyamory — and I hope it sparks enough curiosity to make you want to explore more. I've gathered some of the best resources that I know to help you dive deeper. I hope it will be as fulfilling to you as it has been to me!
